TRIBUTE TEA has selected Baieido Incense as its premium incense line. Baieido was established in 1657 by Kakuuemon Yamatoya, a wholesaler of medicinal herbs in Sakai city. Kakuuemon took the name Jinkoya Sakubei, meaning “aloeswood (jinkoh) trader” and specialized in the sale of incense sticks and raw ingredients.

In the centuries' old tradition of their founder, Baieido has dedicated itself to the production of the highest quality incense for nearly 350 years. Their methods and recipes have been handed down from generation to generation in an unbroken secret oral tradition.

Baieido incense is a “whole herb” product and is not made from extractions, synthetics, dyes, preservatives, oils or fragrances. High quality natural ingredients are pulverized, blended, shaped and dried to create the world’s finest traditionally hand-crafted incense products.
