CHIPATA HOSPICE, ZAMBIA

 

EL Studio Netherlands, in partnership with BKVV Architects of the Netherlands,
was commissioned by the Dutch Chipata Foundation to design a hospice
in the city of Chipata, in the Eastern Province of Zambia.
