David Cho—David Cho is President of AAT Television, the only 24/7 Multi-Asian Television Broadcaster in the Northwest.  He has been in the television industry for over 10 years working in mainstream and ethnic TV. Throughout his television venture, he rapidly gained knowledge of the industry by working in various positions from video production, news, digital content management and ad sales. 

During his tenure and innovative ideas at AAT TV, the Chinese 12 year old daily 2 hour programming grew in the past 4 years into a prominent successful 24 hours Multi-Asian programming on DTV and Cable.  AAT TV is recognized as the Northwest’s only Asian TV broadcast and most highly viewed local Asian TV in Washington State.  

He holds BA in Marketing from Seattle University and Master Degree in Communication from the Digital Media (MCDM) program at University of Washington. He serves on the Steering Committee for SeaBeez.
