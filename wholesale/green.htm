West Lake Dragon Well - A fine Dragon Well tea, one of China's "10 Most Famous", grown in the scenic hills around Hangzhou's West Lake. The beautiful champagne-colored golden liquor has a complex flavor rich with sweet, savory and floral notes and a buttery smooth, long-lasting finish.  
Genmaicha - Literally "brown rice" tea, this Japanese specialty blends green tea and toasted and popped brown rice to create a savory infusion that drinks well throughout the day. A generous dusting of matcha (powdered Japanese green tea) adds a boost of green tea flavor and gives the brew a beautiful bright green color.
