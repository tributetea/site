FLAT RATE SHIPPING

We're not sure why the Post Office does it, but we know we love it. Flat Rate Priority Mail is the best deal in shipping. We can ship six pounds of tea across the country in 2-3 days for just $9. Parcel Post (which would take over a week) would cost $13. (Regular Priority would be $19.) Wondering about UPS? They would charge $12.30 for one-week shipping, $30.99 for 3-Day. Want something shipped to Alaska or Hawaii? Same price from the Post Office; over $40 with UPS.

If you double it (2 flat-rate boxes), that's $17.90. That's still cheaper (and a lot faster) than a 13 pound parcel shipmnet ($18.80) or UPS Ground ($18.24).
