Tribute Tea is pleased to announce a new partnership with the Bai Hu Tang Yixing gallery in Beijing. (Their calligraphy, meaning "Hall of 100 Teapots", appears above.) Headed by Yixing authority Zhou Xiong Wei, Bai Hu Tang specializes in contemporary hand-made artist's teapots produced in strictly limited editions with both the collector and tea drinker in mind. During his career, Mr. Zhou has sold works by National Craft Masters Gu Shao Pei, Li Chang Hong and Bao Zhi Qiang, amongst numerous other regionally and nationally recognized artists. For the Tribute Tea Collector's Edition, Mr. Zhou works with a select group of potters in Yixing, personally supervising every step of the creative process - from design and execution to the final inspection of every pot. Throughout the process it is Mr. Zhou's knowledge, experience and personal passion for Yixing pottery that allow us to offer the highest quality and best value in collector's grade Yixing teapots.
