PRIVACY:
When you place an order with TRIBUTE TEA you are providing us with sensitive personal information such as your name, address, phone number, credit card account details, and so on.
At TRIBUTE TEA we're not just on-line merchants, we're on-line consumers like you, so we share your concerns about internet privacy and security. Therefore, your personal information will be used only to process your order and will never be shared with, sold or otherwise transferred to any other parties for any purpose.

SECURITY:
TRIBUTE TEA uses a Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol to insure the security of the information you send over the internet. An SSL works by encrypting data on your computer and decoding it after it reaches our secure server. As a result your personal information cannot be intercepted and decrypted during transit. Your browser verifies an SSL connection during checkout by displaying a secure URL (web address), prefixed by "https" rather than "http", and a lock icon in the lower right-hand corner. You can click on this icon for security verification.
