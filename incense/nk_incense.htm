The Tokyo-based Nippon Kodo Company is one of the leading incense manufacturers in the world today. Committed to “delivering the message through fragrance”, Nippon Kodo produces a variety of traditional and contemporary incense products.

TRIBUTE TEA has specially selected six unique scents which offer variety, convenience and value to both the tea drinking connoisseur and incense enthusiast alike. Subtle wood and floral aromas, plus exotic Green Tea and Bitter Orange set a tranquil and inviting tone to any tea gathering and add an air of ceremony and sophistication.

Nippon Kodo prides itself on the range and quality of what it describes as its “spiritually inspired fragrances”. Their stated goal is “to bring the culture of incense to the world” and we join them in inviting youn to “listen to the fragrance”.
