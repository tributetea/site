USUCHA (THIN TEA)

Tribute Tea is proud to present two special matchas grown outside the old imperial capital of Kyoto in Uji - long famous for its superior teas. Shading bushes for matcha production is believed to have begun here and Uji teas continue to be the most sought-after for connoisseurship and ceremonial use. After about 3 weeks undercover, the leaves are handpicked, steamed and dried before being stone-ground.

KOICHA (THICK TEA)

 All matcha may be prepared as thin tea using 1-2 tsp. of tea per cup of water. The highest grades, however, may also be prepared as koicha ("thick tea") as shown above. Whisking 6-8 tsp. of matcha in a cup of water produces the ultimate bowl of green tea: the texture is thick and smooth, the color is intense and the flavor is truly unforgettable. Add a little ceremony to your next tea gathering.
