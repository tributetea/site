2008 STAGED PLAY READING SERIES

This series, co-presented with the Elliott Bay Book Company, features new, award-winning and classic works by local, national and international playwrights. These fully staged play readings are a unique blend of the spoken word and theatrical staging. Please join us for these special presentations presented throughout the summer and fall. RABBIT HOLE

By David Lindsay-Abaire

Richard Hugo House

2pm - May 18, 2008

Play Reading

ReAct is thrilled to kick off our 8th Annual Staged Play Reading Series on a grand note with a reading presentation (and preview for our mainstage production next season) of David Lindsay-Abaire's Pulitzer Prize-winning play. RABBIT HOLE Rabbit Hole is the marvelous new play about what comes after tragedy. A story of loss, heartbreak and forgiveness—told through daily moments and emotional hurdles—as a family moves on after the accidental death of their 4-year-old son. After a critically acclaimed recent Broadway premiere, Rabbit Hole has been hailed as an artistic breakthrough for the highly regarded Lindsay-Abaire. We hope you didn't miss this very special event.
